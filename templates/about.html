About

Nolan Gage


For my first project, I created a web-based application for purchasing individual cards for the Magic: the Gathering trading card game. I chose this project because a storefront is a simple web application that can take advantage of different API's for additional features. The three API's that I chose to integrate are Paypal, USPS Tracking, and Scryfall (an API dedicated to retrieval of information on individual MtG cards)
